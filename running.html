Composatron 9000: Output

Below is a working example of the genetic algorithm that would determine the midi and instrument samples used in each song.

Each line represents a song, each pair of numbers representing a midi and an instrument

Users can rate each song (in theory after listening to it) by pressing the number keys, and after rating all ten; press enter to generate a new generation.

new child songs inherit user favored traits, and also have a chance to mutate in order to increase diversity.

You can click in the box below and enter numbers and click enter to generate new populations and test the genetic algorithm yourself. We apologize that no audio was implemented into our final artifact
